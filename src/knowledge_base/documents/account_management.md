# Account Management

## Password Reset
If you've forgotten your password, click "Forgot Password" on the login page. A reset link will be sent to your registered email address within 5 minutes. The link expires after 24 hours. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support.

## Account Lockout
After 5 consecutive failed login attempts, your account will be temporarily locked for 30 minutes. This is a security measure to protect your account. If you believe your account was locked in error, contact our support team for immediate assistance.

## Updating Profile Information
To update your name, email, or phone number, go to Settings > Account > Profile. Email changes require verification via a confirmation link sent to your new email address. Please allow up to 24 hours for changes to propagate across all systems.

## Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
We strongly recommend enabling 2FA for added security. Go to Settings > Security > Two-Factor Authentication. We support authenticator apps (Google Authenticator, Authy) and SMS-based verification. If you lose access to your 2FA device, use one of your backup recovery codes to regain access.

## Account Deletion
To permanently delete your account, go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. This action is irreversible. All your data, including files, preferences, and history, will be permanently removed within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email before the deletion is finalized.
