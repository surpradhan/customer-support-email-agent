# Privacy and Security

## Data Encryption
All data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 and in transit using TLS 1.3. Database backups are also encrypted and stored in geographically distributed data centers.

## Compliance
We are compliant with:
- **SOC 2 Type II** — Audited annually by an independent third party.
- **GDPR** — Full compliance for EU customers, including data portability and right to erasure.
- **CCPA** — California residents can request access to or deletion of their personal data.
- **HIPAA** — Available on Enterprise plans with a signed BAA (Business Associate Agreement).

## Data Retention
- Active accounts: Data is retained for the duration of the subscription.
- Deleted accounts: Data is permanently purged within 30 days of account deletion.
- Logs: System and access logs are retained for 90 days for security purposes.

## Data Export
You can export all your data at any time. Go to Settings > Account > Export Data. Formats available: CSV, JSON, and PDF. Exports are processed in the background and emailed to your registered address within 24 hours.

## Reporting Security Issues
If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it to security@example.com. We have a responsible disclosure program and will acknowledge your report within 24 hours. We do not pursue legal action against good-faith security researchers.

## Third-Party Integrations
When you connect third-party services (Slack, Google Workspace, etc.), we only request the minimum permissions necessary. You can review and revoke integrations at any time from Settings > Integrations. We never sell your data to third parties.
