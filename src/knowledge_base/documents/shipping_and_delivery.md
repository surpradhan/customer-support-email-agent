# Shipping and Delivery

## Digital Delivery
All software subscriptions are delivered digitally. Upon purchase, you'll receive an email with your login credentials and a link to access the platform immediately. No physical shipping is involved for standard subscriptions.

## Hardware Add-Ons
For customers who purchase hardware add-ons (security keys, branded USB drives):
- **Standard Shipping** — Free, 5-7 business days within the US.
- **Express Shipping** — $15, 2-3 business days within the US.
- **International Shipping** — $25-45 depending on location, 7-14 business days.

## Order Tracking
Once your hardware order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. Track your order at orders.example.com or through the carrier's website directly.

## Delivery Issues
- **Package not received**: If your package hasn't arrived within the estimated delivery window, contact us with your order number. We'll investigate with the carrier and either locate the package or send a replacement.
- **Damaged item**: Take a photo of the damage and email it to support@example.com within 48 hours of delivery. We'll send a replacement at no extra cost.
- **Wrong item received**: Contact us immediately. We'll arrange a return label and ship the correct item within 1-2 business days.
