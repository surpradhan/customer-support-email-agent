# Refund Policy

## 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee
All subscription plans come with a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with our service within the first 30 days of your initial subscription, you can request a full refund — no questions asked.

## How to Request a Refund
1. Go to Settings > Billing > Request Refund.
2. Select the reason for your refund (optional but appreciated).
3. Click "Submit Request."
Alternatively, email support@example.com with your account email and the subject line "Refund Request."

## Refund Processing Time
Refunds are reviewed within 1-2 business days. Once approved, the refund will appear on your original payment method within 5-7 business days, depending on your bank or payment provider.

## Partial Refunds
If you cancel your subscription after the 30-day guarantee window, you will not receive a refund for the remaining time on your current billing period. However, you will retain access to all features until the end of that period.

## Annual Plan Refunds
For annual subscriptions, refunds are available within the first 30 days of purchase. After 30 days, you may cancel at any time, but the remaining balance will be converted to account credit that can be used for future billing or transferred to another account.

## Exceptions
Refunds are not available for: add-on purchases (extra storage, premium integrations), professional services (custom setup, training sessions), or accounts terminated due to Terms of Service violations.
