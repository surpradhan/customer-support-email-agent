# Billing and Payments

## Subscription Plans
We offer three plans:
- **Starter** — $9/month: Up to 3 users, 10GB storage, email support.
- **Professional** — $29/month: Up to 15 users, 100GB storage, priority email and chat support.
- **Enterprise** — $99/month: Unlimited users, 1TB storage, dedicated account manager, phone support, SLA guarantee.

All plans include a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start.

## Upgrading or Downgrading
Go to Settings > Billing > Change Plan. Upgrades take effect immediately — you'll be charged the prorated difference for the remainder of your billing cycle. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. Your data will be preserved, but features exclusive to higher plans will become unavailable.

## Payment Methods
We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and PayPal. To update your payment method, go to Settings > Billing > Payment Methods. For annual billing, we also accept bank transfers — contact our sales team for details.

## Invoices and Receipts
Invoices are automatically emailed after each payment. You can also download them from Settings > Billing > Invoice History. All invoices are available in PDF format. For custom invoice requirements (e.g., tax ID, company address), update your billing details in Settings > Billing > Company Info.

## Failed Payments
If a payment fails, we'll retry automatically after 3 days. You'll receive an email notification. After 3 failed attempts, your account will be downgraded to the free tier. To resolve, update your payment method and manually retry from Settings > Billing > Payment Methods. No data will be lost during this period.
